Get in Touch

Kim is an Executive Assistant, Integrator, and Air National Guard Veteran who brings clarity, structure, and calm to fast-moving leaders and teams. With a background spanning education, operations, and executive support, she now partners virtually from North Carolina with an EOS Implementer in Michigan — helping organizations stay aligned, intentional, and moving forward.

"After years of supporting leaders, I kept seeing how often administrative professionals work in isolation. That had to change."

After years of supporting leaders and witnessing how often administrative professionals work in isolation, Kim founded Empower & Elevate — a monthly workshop designed to connect Executive Assistants and Project Managers who want to grow, collaborate, and support one another. Her sessions blend practical tools, honest conversation, and a welcoming community that reflects her signature style: warm, grounded, and refreshingly real.

Whether she's orchestrating executive logistics or facilitating conversations that spark breakthroughs, Kim leads with service, humor, and a deep belief in the power of community.

What drives the community
The Empower & Elevate approach

Practical over theoretical

Every workshop is built around real tools and real situations — nothing generic, nothing filler. If it doesn't help on Monday morning, it doesn't make the cut.

Community over isolation

EAs and PMs do their best work when they're connected. This community exists to end the isolation that too many administrative professionals quietly accept.

Warm and real

No corporate fluff, no performance. Just honest conversation, genuine support, and a space where you can show up as you actually are.
