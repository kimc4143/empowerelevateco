Monthly Workshops

Practical sessions for EAs who want to grow, connect, and support one another. One hour, once a month, always worth it.

How it works
What to expect

Every session follows a consistent format so you always know what you're walking into - and you're always welcome, whether it's your first time or your tenth.

01

Introductions

Every workshop kicks off with one-minute intros. A quick, low-pressure way to meet who's in the room and connect with EAs across the EOS ecosystem.

02

Deep-Dive

Each session has various topics - tools, workflows, software deep-dives, or timely conversations about the EA role. Practical, relevant, and built around real situations.

03

Community & Connection

Time to talk, share, and just be around people who get it. The community is the point - Kim keeps the conversation warm, honest, and refreshingly real.
