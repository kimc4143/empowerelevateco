Using the Platform

EOS has migrated the resource library from Dropbox to Google Drive, making it easier to find and download everything in one place. Files are still available as hyperlinks throughout the Academy, but you can now browse and download the entire collection directly.

How to Access
Access the File Library: Click Here

The Main Hub: Look for the "Implementer Academy Files" space inside the Practice Management space group.

The Quick Link: Click the "File Library" link in the menu at the very top of the Academy page.

Note: Master files are view-only. To customize a template, download it to your device and edit your personal copy.
Shared by the Empower & Elevate community

Once your Quarterly is complete and you're ready to archive the completed Rocks for that quarter, here's how to do it in Ninety.

Steps
1. Navigate to the Team's Rocks page in Ninety.

2. Click the ellipses (...) button at the top right of the filter bar.

3. Select Archive Completed from the dropdown menu.

Shared by Jordyn, Ninety Digital Success Team
